Overnight Oats

Personal recipe

Ingredients

  • Old Fashion Oats
  • Cinnamon
  • Yogurt (anykind of yogurt you prefer)
  • Honey
  • Milk
  • Blueberrys
  • a Mason Jar

Steps

  1. Pour oats in mason jar
  2. Pour in a small amount of cinnamon
  3. Add in blueberrys
  4. Add a decent amount of yogurt
  5. add 1 cup milk (you can put more or less)
  6. Mix
  7. Store in fridge or eat as is
