Beef Stew (Instant Pot)

Delicious and Nutritious

Ingredients

  • 1lb Beef
  • 1tbsp Soy sauce
  • 1lb Carrots
  • 12 oz Potatoes
  • 1lb Green Beans
  • 2 cups Beef broth
  • 1 tsp Dried thyme
  • 1 tsp Rosemary
  • 2 tbsp Flour
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Place the stew meat in a bowl and season lightly with salt and pepper. Add the flour and stir to coat
  2. Place the butter in the Instant Pot and turn on the saut√© function. Heat the butter until it is melted and sizzling, then add the stew meat.
  3. Brown the meat on all sides. Don't stir too often as this will prevent the meat from achieving a nice brown, crispy exterior.
  4. Pour the beef broth into the pot and stir to dissolve the browned bits from the bottom of the pot. Once dissolved, add the Worcestershire sauce, soy sauce, brown sugar, minced garlic, tomato paste, rosemary, and thyme.
  5. Add carrots,potatoes and green beans
  6. Close the lid on the Instant Pot and close the steam valve. Press the "meat/stew" button, or set the cooker to 35 minutes of high pressure.
  7. After 35 minutes of high pressure, allow the cooker to naturally cool and release pressure.
  8. Bon appetit
